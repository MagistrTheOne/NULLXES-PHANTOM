PHANTOM is a from-scratch foundation model program.
The flagship targets strong performance in code, math, long-form reasoning, and technical instruction following.
Tokenizer training should preserve whitespace, indentation, JSON, XML, Markdown, URLs, SQL, file paths, and stack traces.
The model weights, tokenizer vocabulary, merges, and training data are owned and produced in-house.
Long-context stages should progress from 4K to 32K, then 128K and 256K.
